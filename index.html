Sangonomiya Kokomi

the young Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island and a descendant of the Sangonomiya Clan, as well as in charge of most of Watatsumi's affairs, shouldering heavy responsibilities alone in hopes for giving Watatsumi Island's people the hopes and happiness that they desire.


being born in a respected family, kokomi is naturally well-educated. she reads a lot of books and is mostly in love with military books. kokomi often visits Yae Publishing House to get the newest books of this genre. her favorite, Teyvat Art of War, was also purchased there. this book covers classic military campaigns from famous strategists.

curiosities

  1. kokomi's family surname, Sangonomiya, means "coral sanctuary", which completely lives up to the clan's location and Watatsumi Island environment.
  2. Dracaena Somnolenta, the name of kokomi's Constellation, is a tribute/reference to the Sleeping Dragon, Zhuge Liang, a hero and master strategist.
  3. kokomi was born on february 22nd, which corresponds to the sign of pisces, indicating a clear connection with her abilities.
  4. kokomi is the Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island, whose title refers to the one who knows all the inhabitants and everything related to the business on the island.
  5. she also holds the title of Pearl of Wisdom, as indicated through her banner.
